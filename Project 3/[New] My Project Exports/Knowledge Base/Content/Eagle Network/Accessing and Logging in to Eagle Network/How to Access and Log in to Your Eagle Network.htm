In order to reach Eagle Network, students must sign in first. This process is very different from how other UNT services and pages operate.

There are two ways to sign in to Eagle Network, one method is logging directly while the second is logging via MyUNT.

 

This section will describe how students can access and log in to Eagle Network through different routes.

 

Accessing and Logging in to Eagle Network

Method 1: Log in Directly through Eagle Network

1. In your browser, type in studentaffairs.unt.edu/eagle-network

2. Press Enter.

The page should appear as followed

 

3. Scroll down to the Login Directly to Eagle Network section

4. Fill in your EUID and Eagle Network password

Note: Through this method, you will have to create or use a different password than the one you have chosen for your MyUNT, EagleConnect, and Blackboard.

 

Method 2: Log in Through MyUNT

1. Type in studentaffairs.unt.edu/eagle-network into your browser.

2. Press Enter

The below page should appear

 

3. Scroll down to the Login to Eagle Network via myUNT section.

4. Fill in your EUID and MyUNT password as followed

5. Click Login
