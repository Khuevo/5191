Students may access the My Account option on Eagle Network to create their Profile, upload and manage all their Documents on the service

(resume, cover letter, application, references, transcripts, etc.), connect with UNT Career Center partners in your Connections (like internships.com,

College Recruiter, etc., and keep track of your Activity including referrals, placements, schedules, and Event RSVPs.

 

This section will describe how to access and use the below:

  • My Profile
  • My Documents
  • My Connections
  • My Activity

 

Accessing and Editing Your Profile

1. Click on My Account

 

2. Click on My Profile on the dropdown menu.

The below page should appear

 

3. Edit your information by clicking on the Edit link

4. Click Save to finish your edits

 

Accessing and Using My Documents

1. Click on My Account

2. Click on My Documents

The below page should appear

 

3. Click Add on each section to browse and upload your documents

Note: You can add more than one resume, cover letter, other documents, but you can only have one Student Employment Application at once.

4. Browse your file from your computer and name the file

5. Click Save to complete the upload

 

How to Access and Use My Connections

1. Click on My Account

2. Click on My Connections on the dropdown menu

The below page should appear

3. Select Yes or No upon your preference to connect to UNT Career Center partners

4. Click Submit
