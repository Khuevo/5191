When you select a course on the My Institution page, you will automatically be taken to the Announcements section for that course. You can

return to this page by either clicking the Announcements option in the Course Menu or by clicking Home icon next to the course title

(also in the Course Menu)

 

Announcements are listed in reverse chronological order. The Announcements page is important to check regularly because instructors will

often announce important information pertaining to the course. Instructors may post important links, assignment reminders, extra credit opportunities.
