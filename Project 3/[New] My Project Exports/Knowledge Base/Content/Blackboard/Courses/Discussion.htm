The Discussions page offers a discussion board or forum that members of a class can create and contribute to. Some classes require you to post on

Blackboard’s discussion board to discuss readings or other assignments.

Once a Forum has been created, you can open it and read the Threads written inside the Forum. You can then reply to any responses made inside

of a thread.

 

Creating a Forum

1. On the Discussions page, click the Create Forum button

 

2. Insert a title for your post in the Name field

3. Type your discussion in the Description field

4. Click Submit

 

Replying to a Thread within a Forum

1. Once you have selected a forum, click the title of the Thread.

Note: The title is blue

 

 

2. Click the blue Reply button in the bottom left of the post that you want to comment on

 

3. Type your response in the Message field

 

Note: The Subject field will automatically be named, “RE: (name of thread you’re replying to)," but you can change this field if you want your reply to have a different subject.

You may also attach a file by clicking "Browse My Computer," and selecting a file.

4. Click Submit
