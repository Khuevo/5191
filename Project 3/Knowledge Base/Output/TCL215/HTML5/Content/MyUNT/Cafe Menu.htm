The menu for any of the Cafeterias on campus can be viewed by clicking on the name of a hall listed in the Cafeteria Menus box.

This box is located on the right side of MyUNT’s home screen and is the second box from the bottom.

 

When you click on one of the hall names a new tab will open with the menu for the next couple of weeks. You can select what day’s menu

you want to view by using the navigation pane on the left side of the screen. The menu is broken down into what food is available at breakfast,

lunch and dinner. If you are allergic to something and are unsure if a meal contains an ingredient that you’re allergic to, you can:

1. Click the Set Allergen Filters button located directly above the menu food lists.

  • A popup menu will appear

2. Click the boxes of any foods that you are allergic to.
