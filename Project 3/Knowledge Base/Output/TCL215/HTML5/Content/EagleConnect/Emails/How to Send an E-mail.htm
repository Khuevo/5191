This section will cover one of the most important aspects of EagleConnect: how to send an e-mail. In order to survive college, students will have to send

many e-mails to professors and other students. This section describes how to do so both through the computer and Smartphone versions of

EagleConnect.

 

How to Send E-mails

Computer

1. On the main page, select New

 

The page should appear as followed:

 

2. Fill in each section of the e-mail:

• To: E-mail of the receiver

• CC: E-mail(s) of the further receiver(s)

• Add a subject: Brief subject of the content of the e-mail. Should be short and precise

• Content is underneath the previous sections

 

3. Click Send to send the e-mail to the receiver(s) as followed:

 

SmartPhone

1. Open the app as you have already signed in

2. Touch + to create a new e-mail as followed

 

The page should appear as followed

3. Fill in each section of the e-mail:

• To: E-mail of the receiver. The + is used to add the e-mail address of people on the phone contact list

• CC: E-mail(s) of the further receiver(s)

• Add a subject: Brief subject of the content of the e-mail. Should be short and precise

• Content is underneath the previous sections

 

4. Select Send to send e-mail.
