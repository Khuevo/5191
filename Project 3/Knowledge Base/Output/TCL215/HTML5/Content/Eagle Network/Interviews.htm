Students can select the Interviews option to see their interview schedules. Students must set these schedules in order to view them online.

Students can choose to sign-up for the interviews they meet the requirements of by using the option Sign-up for Interviews I’m Qualified For.

They can also choose to View All Upcoming Interviews.

 

Searching for Interviews Students Qualify for

1. Click on Interviews

 

2. Click on Sign-up for Interviews I’m Qualified For.

The page should appear as followed

 

3. Type in keywords in the Keyword section and then click Search.

 

4. Select Advanced Search and choose more options upon preference

Viewing All Upcoming Interviews

1. Click on Interviews

2. Click on View All Upcoming Interviews

The page should appear as followed:

 

2. Type in keywords in the Keyword section and then click Search

 

3. Click on Advanced Search and choose more options upon preference
