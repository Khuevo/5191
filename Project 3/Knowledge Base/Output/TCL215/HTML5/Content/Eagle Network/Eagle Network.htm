Eagle Network is a referral service offered to students and employers at the University of North Texas (UNT).

 

Students

Students may access the latest information about available opportunities for on- and off-campus jobs and internships. Students may also sign up for

interviews they think they are qualified for. Students can also sign up for events and workshops held by Career Center at UNT using this service.

 

Employers

Eagle Network offers employers the opportunities to create job postings and register for events. On- and off-campus recruiters may post job

advertisements, but they must comply with the University of North Texas Nondiscrimination/Equal Employment Opportunity and Affirmative Action

Policy (1.3.7) and the university’s additional Recruiting Policies and Guidelines. Employers can also sign up for events like job fairs, where they have

the chance to meet students on campus to find the best matches for the company.

 

Accessing Eagle Network

To access this service, students must sign in to the page using their Enterprise-Wide User-ID (EUID, which is tied to each UNT student, and they can

choose to access the service via MyUNT or log in directly to the page. However, they must use a different password for their EUID if students choose

to log in directly to Eagle Network. Eagle Network requires a separate password with MyUNT, Blackboard, and EagleConnect.
