You are here: Blackboard > My Institutions

The My Institution page is the first page you will see after logging into Blackboard. This screen displays modules with information from your courses.

Below is a list of the modules on the My Institution page.

To Do

A list of all assignment that are due in the near future as well as assignments that were due in the recent past. You can organize upcoming due dates by expanding any of the four options menus under the What's Due section. The options in the What's Due section are, Today, Tomorrow, This Week and Future. This can be a valuable reference tool when you need to know how soon to turn in an assignment.

My Courses

 

A list of the classes that you are enrolled in. Classes that do not have a blackboard site set up will not be listed in this menu

Tools

Contains the following tools:

  •  Announcements
  • Personal Information
  • Calendar
  • On Demand Learning Center
  • Send Email
  • Tasks
  • My Grades

My Announcements

Recent announcements from all of your courses will appear here in chronological order.

Browser Check

Checks to make sure that your browser is running the required elements (JavaScript and Cookies)

Student Helpdesk Support

Provides contact information via a phone number and a physical address that you can contact if you are having difficulty with Blackboard. The support hours for the Student Helpdesk are also listed.
