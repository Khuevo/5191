The Finances button on the navigation drops down the options to Make a Payment, Enroll in a Payment Plan, and check your Account Balance

Making a Payment

1. Click the Make a Payment button

2. Click the Make Payment by Credit Card or eCheck button

3. Click the Pay Bill Button

You will be redirected to UNT’s QuikPay™ website where you can make your payment, check your transaction history, change your preference and authorize other payers.

Checking Account Balance

1. Click the Account Balance button

The default view is of the Account Overview tab, but you can choose to look at the Term Summary and the Charges due by clicking the corresponding tabs.
