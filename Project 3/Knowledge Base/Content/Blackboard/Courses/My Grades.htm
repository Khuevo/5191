My Grades

Students may use My Grades to check their grades. On the My Grades page, students can choose to view All, Graded, Upcoming or Submitted assignments.

The first two grades on the page (while in the All category) are the Weighted Total and the Total amount of points you’ve earned in the class. The Weighted

Total is sometimes only available once all assignments have been graded and assigned a “weight." The weight of a grade varies depending on how

important the assignment is compared to other assignments. For example, a daily grade typically weighs less than a test grade. If assignments are graded on

the same point scale for each assignment but the weights differ, the Total Points score may be misleading.

 

Below the two totals, there is a list of all the assignments that have been given throughout the semester. The list starts with the first grade taken in the class and ends

with the most recent. Teachers often leave comments when they put your grade in Blackboard. These comments are useful because they inform you of what you did right or

wrong in the assignment which teaches you what to do in future assignments to sustain or improve your grade.

 

Clicking the Graded button will show you a list of only assignments that have already been graded. If you want to check to see when an assignment is due, you can click

the Upcoming button at the top of the page. If you’re unsure if you turned in an assignment because you haven’t received a grade for it yet, you can click the Submitted button

to see a list of assignments that have been turned in but not graded.
