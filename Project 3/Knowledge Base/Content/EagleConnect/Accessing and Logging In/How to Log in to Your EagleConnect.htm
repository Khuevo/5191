There are various ways to sign in to e-mail via EagleConnect both on computer and smartphone.

 

Computer

1. On the EagleConnect page, click Sign In to EagleConnect

The page should appear as followed:

 

2. Type in your UNT e-mail/EUID and your corresponding password.

3. Click Sign in.

 

Smartphone

Before completing the steps in this section, ensure you have completed the steps to set up EagleConnect on your Smartphone. This section can be found here.

Both Apple and Android users may follow the same below steps to sign in to their EagleConnect.

 

1. Once on the sign in page, type in your UNT email/EUID and corresponding password in the appropriate blanks.

 

2. Click Sign in.
