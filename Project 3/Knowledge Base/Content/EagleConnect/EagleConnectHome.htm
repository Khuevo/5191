EagleConnect is an e-mail service exclusively used at the University of North Texas (UNT). All students, employees, and alumni can use this service.

This e-mail service runs on Outlook. Students, faculty, and staff can access their e-mails through a computer or on smartphones using the Outlook application on

Apple and Android (at eagleconnect.unt.edu/mobile).

 

Features

EagleConnect offers users the following features:

  • Antivirus and anti-spam protection with automatic encryption
  • Shared address book and calendar information
  • 50 GB of storage shared with mailbox and OneDrive
  • Free MS Office Web Apps and Office 365 ProPlus
  • 25 Languages
  • Chat

 

Students often use this email address for any school related online correspondence. This address is tied to each student’s Enterprise-Wide User-ID (EUID) and is

publicly available via the Student, Faculty, and Staff directory. This e-mail address is the default address that professors are given to contact students.

Once signed in, users can find the basic functions of an e-mail service. EagleConnect allows users to access an inbox, clutter, sent items, drafts, etc.

Users can start a new e-mail, delete or archive e-mails, and move and categorize e-mails. The e-mail service also provides users with the option to create Groups,

where people can connect to one another.

 

Users can also change their display themes, create automatic responses, insert signatures in every e-mail they send, and perform even more functions.
