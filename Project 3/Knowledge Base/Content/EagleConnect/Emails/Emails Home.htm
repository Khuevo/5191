E-mail is the fundamental function of EagleConnect, the service exclusively supplied by University of North Texas (UNT). This address is tied to each

student’s Enterprise-Wide User-ID (EUID) and is publicly available via the Student, Faculty, and Staff directory. This e-mail address is the default

address that professors are given to contact students.

 

Once signed in, users access multiples resources including the following:

• Inbox

• Sent

• Clutter

• Drafts

• Groups

• Folders

Students can start a new e-mail, delete or archive e-mails, and move and categorize e-mails. The e-mail service also provides users with the option to

create Groups, where people can connect to one another.
