Students can send documents, photos, and more through e-mail by attaching these files to the e-mail.

 

This section describes how students can add attachments to their e-mails on the computer and Smartphone versions of EagleConnect.

 

Attaching Files in E-mails

Computer

1. When creating a new e-mail, select Attach.

2. Select your file via OneDrive or from a location on your computer.

 

3. Choose how you would like to attach your file.

The options are shown below.

 

Smartphone

1. While creating a new e-mail, click + as shown below

 

2. Select Insert.

 

 

3. Select Attachments or OneDrive files to attach documents.

Note: You may choose to attach pictures as well.

 

4. Choose the file(s) to attach
